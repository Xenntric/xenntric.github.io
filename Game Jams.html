Stork Market

Mobile/Sandbox

Stork Market is a flash-like baby building game where you receive requests for hand designed babies. The player stitches a baby together out of body parts on a conveyor belt and after assembly, babies are assessed by how well they met the request. This was our second project where I created the art assets and outline shader, our task was to create a game based off of a randomised prompt; 'A game where you distribute babies to dominate the market'.

Dranc

Puzzle/Adventure

Dranc was created both as university work and a submission for the 2022 Global Game Jam. Dranc was created by a team of 9 people, 6 programmers and 3 artists, and was our introduction to collaborating with people with limited programming knowledge, and we had to drastically change our approach of game development. I created the final level and was the primary link to communicating with the different artists supporting our game.

Stumble Chums

Arcade

Stumble Chums is an Atari 2600-style demake of Fall Guys. This was our first 3rd year project where we worked in a team of 5, focusing on getting us aquainted with rapid prototyping and turnover with a limit of 2 weeks per game. It features a side-on interpretation of the Jump Club minigame to demonstrate an increasing difficulty curve.

Flynn Starchad

Mobile/Action

Made for the 2019 Global Game Jam with the theme of 'Repair', with the only other stipulation was that it had to be playable on mobile. Flynn Starchad is a one-touch mobile game where the player has a double-barrelled shotgun which alternates between a blast that destroys and repairs.
