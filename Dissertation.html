Mushroom Generation Project


This was my most successful project from my Advanced Technology module during 3rd year. With the aim of making a system that can simulate the growth of the inky cap mushroom, this system allows for control over the speed of growth, whether the mushrooms will grow in a cluster or colony, and options to randomise direction, hight, and stem widths.

Mushrooms will grow following a spline dictated by quadratic bezier curve and will change stage at a user-set percentage of growth. Mushrooms are also placed along a grid using Poisson Disk Sampling to give a realistic distribution for the colony.
